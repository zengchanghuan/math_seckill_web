11. 【解析】

方法一：  $\lim_{n\to \infty}n[(\ln (2 + \frac{1}{n}) - \ln 2] = \lim_{n\to \infty}n\ln (1 + \frac{1}{2n})$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \ln (1 + \frac {1}{2 n}) ^ {n} \\ = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \ln [ (1 + \frac {1}{2 n}) ^ {2 n} ] ^ {\frac {1}{2}} \\ = \ln e ^ {\frac {1}{2}} \\ = \frac {1}{2} \\ \end{array}
$$

方法二：  $\lim_{n\to \infty}n\left\{(\ln (2 + \frac{1}{n}) - \ln 2\right\} = \lim_{n\to \infty}\frac{\ln(2 + \frac{1}{n}) - \ln 2}{\frac{1}{n}}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = (\ln x) ^ {\prime} \Big | _ {x = 2} \\ = \left. \frac {1}{x} \right| _ {x = 2} = \frac {1}{2} \\ \end{array}
$$

方法三：  $\lim_{n\to \infty}n\left\{(\ln (2 + \frac{1}{n}) - \ln 2\right\} = \lim_{n\to \infty}x\left\{\ln (2 + \frac{1}{x}) - \ln 2\right\}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \lim  _ {n \to \infty} \frac {\ln (2 + \frac {1}{x}) - \ln 2}{\frac {1}{x}} \\ \frac {1}{(2 + \frac {1}{-})} \cdot \left(- \frac {1}{x ^ {2}}\right) \\ = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {x}{- \frac {1}{x ^ {2}}} \\ = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {1}{2 + \frac {1}{x}} \\ = \frac {1}{2} \\ \end{array}
$$

(说明:不转换成函数极限, 直接用洛必达法则计算可以不扣分)

$$
\begin{array}{l} \therefore \frac {d y}{d x} = y ^ {\prime} = \frac {x}{e ^ {y} \sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}} - y} = \frac {x}{e ^ {2 y} - y} \\ \therefore \frac{dy}{dx}\bigg|_{\substack{y = 0\\ x = 1}} = 1 \\ \end{array}

$$
\begin{array}{l} y = \frac {1}{2} \ln \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) \\ \therefore y ^ {\prime} = \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {2 x + 2 y y ^ {\prime}}{x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}} \\ \end{array}

$$
F _ {x} ^ {\prime} = e ^ {y} - \frac {2 y}{2 \sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}} = e ^ {y} - \frac {y}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}},

$$
