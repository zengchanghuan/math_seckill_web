11. 解：原式  $= \lim_{x\to 0}\frac{x\arctan{x}}{3x^2}$
12. 解：  $\because \frac{\mathrm{d}^2y}{\mathrm{d}x^2} = \left(\frac{1}{2}\ln x + \sqrt{\ln x} + 2\ln 2\right)^2$
$$
\begin{array}{l} = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {\arctan x}{3 x} = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {\frac {1}{1 + x ^ {2}}}{3} = \frac {1}{3}. \\ = \frac {1}{2 x} + \frac {1}{2 x \sqrt {\ln x}}, \\ \therefore \frac {\mathrm {d} ^ {2} y}{\mathrm {d} x ^ {2}} \Bigg | _ {x = \mathrm {e}} = \frac {1}{2 \mathrm {e}} + \frac {1}{2 \mathrm {e}} = \frac {1}{\mathrm {e}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

（2）  $f^{\prime \prime}(x) = \frac{-ab^{2}\mathrm{e}^{b\cdot x}\left(1 - \mathrm{e}^{b\cdot x}\right)}{\left(1 + \mathrm{e}^{b\cdot x}\right)^{3}},$

$$

$$
