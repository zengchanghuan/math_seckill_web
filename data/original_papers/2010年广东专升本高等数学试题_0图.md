11. 解：原式  $= \lim_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} \frac{\cot x}{-4(\pi - 2x)}$

$$
= \lim  _ {x \rightarrow \frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {- \csc^ {2} x}{8} = - \frac {1}{8}
$$

12. 解:  $f^{\prime}(0) = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(\Delta x) - f(0)}{\Delta x} = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\Delta x^{2} \sin \frac{2}{\Delta x} + \sin 2\Delta x}{\Delta x}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \lim  _ {\Delta x \rightarrow 0} (\Delta x \sin \frac {2}{\Delta x} + \frac {\sin 2 \Delta x}{\Delta x}) = 0 + \lim  _ {\Delta x \rightarrow 0} \frac {\sin 2 \Delta x}{\Delta x} \\ = 2 \lim  _ {\Delta x \rightarrow 0} \frac {\sin 2 \Delta x}{2 \Delta x} = 2 \\ \end{array}
$$

13. 解：由题意知  $ae + b = 1$  ①

又因为  $y^\prime = -\frac{a}{x^2} e^{\frac{1}{x}} + 2bx,y'' = \frac{2a}{x^3} e^{\frac{1}{x}} + \frac{a}{x^4} e^{\frac{1}{x}} + 2b$

所以，由题意知  $2ae + ae + 2b = 3ae + 2b = 0$  (2)

由①和②解得  $a = -\frac{2}{e}, b = 3$

14. 解一：原式  $= \int \frac{\cos x(1 + \cos x)}{\sin^2 x} dx = \int \frac{\cos x}{\sin^2 x} dx + \int \cot^2 x dx$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \int \frac {1}{\sin^ {2} x} d \sin x + \int \left(\csc^ {2} x - 1\right) d x \\ = - \frac {1}{\sin x} - \cot x - x + C \\ \end{array}
$$

解二：原式  $= \int \frac{1 - 2\sin^2\frac{x}{2}}{2\sin^2\frac{x}{2}} dx$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{2} \int \csc^ {2} \frac {x}{2} d x - \int d x = \int \csc^ {2} \frac {x}{2} d \left(\frac {x}{2}\right) - \int d x \\ = - \cot \frac {x}{2} - x + C \\ \end{array}
$$

15. 解：令  $\sqrt{e^x - 1} = t$ ，则  $x = \ln (1 + t^2), dx = \frac{2t}{1 + t^2} dt$

所以  $\int_{\ln 5}^{\ln 10}\sqrt{e^x - 1} dx = \int_2^3\frac{2t^2dt}{1 + t^2} = 2\int_2^3 dt - 2\int_2^3\frac{dt}{1 + t^2}$

$$
= 2 - 2 (\arctan 3 - \arctan 2)
$$

16. 解:  $y = e^{-\int \frac{1}{x} dx} \left( \int \sin x e^{\int \frac{1}{x} dx} dx + C \right)$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = e ^ {- \ln x} \left(\int \sin x e ^ {\ln x} d x + C\right) \\ = \frac {1}{x} (- x \cos x + \int \cos x d x + C) \\ = - \cos x + \frac {\sin x}{x} + \frac {C}{x} \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = e ^ {- \ln x} \left(\int \sin x e ^ {\ln x} d x + C\right) \\ = \frac {1}{x} (- x \cos x + \int \cos x d x + C) \\ = - \cos x + \frac {\sin x}{x} + \frac {C}{x} \\ \end{array}
$$

17．解：设  $F(x,y,z) = x^{z} - xy^{2} + z^{3} - 1$  ，则

$$
F _ {x} ^ {\prime} = z x ^ {z - 1} - y ^ {2}, F _ {y} ^ {\prime} = - 2 x y, F _ {z} ^ {\prime} = x ^ {z} \ln x + 3 z ^ {2}
$$

所以  $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = -\frac{F_x'}{F_z'} = \frac{y^2 - zx^{z-1}}{x^z\ln x + 3z^2}, \frac{\partial x}{\partial y} = -\frac{F_y'}{F_z'} = \frac{2xy}{x^z\ln x + 3z^2}$

18. 解：积分区域 D 如图：（略）

解方程组  $\left\{ \begin{array}{l} y = x^2 + 1, \\ y = 2x \end{array} \right.$  可求得交点为（1，2）

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {D} 2 x y d x d y = \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x \int_ {2 x} ^ {x ^ {2} + 1} 2 x y d y \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {1} x y ^ {2} \left| \begin{array}{c} x ^ {2} + 1 \\ 2 x \end{array} \right. d x = \int_ {0} ^ {1} x \left(x ^ {4} + 1 - 2 x ^ {2}\right) d x \\ = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {1} (x ^ {2} - 1) ^ {2} d x ^ {2} = \frac {1}{6} (x ^ {2} - 1) ^ {3} \left| \begin{array}{l} 1 \\ 0 \end{array} \right. = \frac {1}{6} \\ \end{array}
$$
