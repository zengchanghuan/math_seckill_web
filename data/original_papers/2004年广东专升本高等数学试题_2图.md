11.【解析】原式  $= \lim_{x\to 0}\frac{e^{x}(x - 2) + e^{x} + 1}{3x^{2}}$

$$
= \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {x e ^ {x}}{6 x}

$$

12.【解析】把  $y$  看成  $x$  的函数并对和方程关于  $x$  求导，得

$$
1 - y ^ {\prime} (x) + \frac {1}{2} \cos y \bullet y ^ {\prime} (x) = 0 \Rightarrow y ^ {\prime} (x) = \frac {1}{1 - \frac {1}{2} \cos y}
$$

再一次求导，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} - y ^ {\prime \prime} (x) + \frac {1}{2} \cos y \bullet y ^ {\prime \prime} (x) - \frac {1}{2} \sin y \bullet \left(y ^ {\prime} (x)\right) ^ {2} = 0 \Rightarrow y ^ {\prime \prime} (x) = - \frac {1}{2} \frac {\sin y \bullet \left(y ^ {\prime} (x)\right) ^ {2}}{1 - \frac {1}{2} \cos y} \\ = - \frac {1}{2} \frac {\sin y}{\left(1 - \frac {1}{2} \cos y\right) ^ {3}} = \frac {x - y}{\left(1 - \frac {1}{2} \cos y\right) ^ {3}} \\ \end{array}
$$

13.【解析】  $\int_0^1 x^5\ln^2 xdx$  令  $e^{t} = x\int_{-\infty}^{0}e^{6t}\bullet t^{2}dt$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{6} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} t ^ {2} d \left(e ^ {6 t}\right) = \frac {1}{6} t ^ {2} e ^ {6 t} \Big | _ {- \infty} ^ {0} - \frac {1}{6} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {6 t} d \left(t ^ {2}\right) = - \frac {1}{3} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {6 t} \bullet t d t \\ = - \frac {1}{1 8} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} t d \left(e ^ {6 t}\right) = - \frac {1}{1 8} t e ^ {6 t} \Big | _ {- \infty} ^ {0} + \frac {1}{1 8} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {6 t} d t = \frac {1}{1 8} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {6 t} d t = \frac {1}{1 0 8} e ^ {6 t} \Big | _ {- \infty} ^ {0} \\ = \frac {1}{1 0 8} \\ \end{array}
$$

14.【解析】  $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \ln (xy) + x\bullet \frac{1}{xy}\bullet y = \ln (xy) + 1$

$$
\frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = x \bullet \frac {1}{x y} \bullet x = \frac {x}{y}
$$

$$
\frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x ^ {2}} = \frac {\partial}{\partial x} (\frac {\partial z}{\partial x}) = \frac {\partial}{\partial x} (\ln (x y) + 1) = \frac {y}{x y} = \frac {1}{x}
$$

$$
\frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x \partial y} = \frac {\partial}{\partial y} (\frac {\partial z}{\partial x}) = \frac {\partial}{\partial y} (\ln (x y) + 1) = \frac {x}{x y} = \frac {1}{y}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} y (x) = x ^ {2} \\ \therefore \quad y ^ {\prime} (x) = 2 x \\ \end{array}
$$

于是过点 c 的切线斜率为  $2x_{0}$ , 其中  $0 \leq x_{0} \leq 8$

$\cdot \cdot$  切线方程为:  $S - x_{0}^{2} = 2x_{0}\left(t - x_{0}\right)$ ,

即  $S = 2x_{0}t - x_{0}^{2}$

此切线与  $y = 0$  和  $x = 8$  分别交于点  $P\left(\frac{x_0}{2}, 0\right)$  和  $Q\left(8, 16x_0 - x_0^2\right)$

$\cdot \cdot$  所围三角形面积  $h$  为

$$
h \left(x _ {0}\right) = \frac {1}{2} \left(8 - \frac {x _ {0}}{2}\right) \left(1 6 x _ {0} - x _ {0} ^ {2}\right)
$$

即  $h(x_0) = \frac{1}{4} x_0 (16 - x_0^2), 0 \leq x_0 \leq 8$

对  $h$  求导，得

$$
h ^ {\prime} \left(x _ {0}\right) = \frac {1}{4} \left(1 6 - x _ {0}\right) ^ {2} - \frac {1}{2} x _ {0} \left(1 6 - x _ {0}\right) = \frac {1}{4} \left(1 6 - x _ {0}\right) \left(1 6 - 3 x _ {0}\right)

$$

$$

下面计算  $\int_0^2 y\sqrt{2y - y^2} dy$

令  $y = 1 + \sin \theta$  ，则当  $\left. \begin{array}{l} y = 0 \text{时}, \theta = -\frac{\pi}{2}, \\ y = 2 \text{时}, \theta = \frac{\pi}{2}, \end{array} \right\} \Rightarrow -\frac{\pi}{2} \leq \theta \leq \frac{\pi}{2}$

于是

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {2} y \sqrt {2 y - y ^ {2}} d y = \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} (1 + \sin \theta) \sqrt {1 - \sin^ {2} \theta} d (1 + \sin \theta) \\ = \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} (1 + \sin \theta) \cos^ {2} \theta d \theta = \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {2} \theta d \theta + \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin \theta \cos^ {2} \theta d \theta \\ = \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {2} \theta d \theta + 0 = \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {1 + \cos 2 \theta}{2} d \theta = \frac {\pi}{2} \\ \end{array}
$$
