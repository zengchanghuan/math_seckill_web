11. 【解析】  $\lim_{n\to \infty}(\sqrt{n^2 + n} -\sqrt{n^2 + 1})$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {n - 1}{\sqrt {n ^ {2} + n} + \sqrt {n ^ {2} + 1}} \\ = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {1 - \frac {1}{n}}{\sqrt {1 + \frac {1}{n}} + \sqrt {1 + \frac {1}{n ^ {2}}}} = \frac {1}{2} \\ \end{array}
$$

12. 【解析】

$$
\begin{array}{l} \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {\int_ {0} ^ {x} \ln^ {2} (1 + t) d t}{x ^ {2}} = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {\left(\int_ {0} ^ {x} \ln^ {2} (1 + t) d t\right) ^ {\prime}}{\left(x ^ {2}\right) ^ {\prime}} \\ = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {\ln^ {2} (1 + x)}{2 x} = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {\left(\ln^ {2} (1 + x)\right) ^ {\prime}}{\left(2 x\right) ^ {\prime}} \\ = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {\frac {2 \ln (1 + x)}{1 + x}}{2} = 0 \\ \end{array}
$$

13. 【解析】  $y' = \left(\arctan \sqrt{x^2 - 1}\right)' - \left(\frac{\ln x}{\sqrt{x^2 - 1}}\right)'.$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{1 + x ^ {2} - 1} \left(\sqrt {x ^ {2} - 1}\right) ^ {\prime} - \frac {\frac {1}{x} \sqrt {x ^ {2} - 1} - \left(\sqrt {x ^ {2} - 1}\right) ^ {\prime} \ln x}{x ^ {2} - 1} \\ = \frac {2 x}{2 x ^ {2} \sqrt {x ^ {2} - 1}} - \frac {1}{x \sqrt {x ^ {2} - 1}} + \frac {\frac {2 x}{2 \sqrt {x ^ {2} - 1}} \ln x}{x ^ {2} - 1} \\ = \frac {x \ln x}{\left(x ^ {2} - 1\right) ^ {3 / 2}} \\ \end{array}
$$

14.【解析】解法一：设  $F(x,y) = \arctan{\frac{y}{x}} -\ln{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}}$  ，则

$$
F _ {x} ^ {\prime} (x, y) = \frac {1}{1 + \left(\frac {y}{x}\right) ^ {2}} \left(- \frac {y}{x ^ {2}}\right) - \frac {1}{2} \frac {2 x}{x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}} = - \frac {x + y}{x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} F _ {y} ^ {\prime} (x, y) = \frac {1}{1 + \left(\frac {y}{x}\right) ^ {2}} \left(\frac {1}{x}\right) - \frac {1}{2} \frac {2 y}{x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}} \\ = \frac {x - y}{x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}} \\ \end{array}
$$

故  $\frac{dy}{dx} = -\frac{F_x'(x,y)}{F_y'(x,y)}$

$$
= \frac {x + y}{x - y} (x \neq y)
$$

方法二：方程  $\arctan{\frac{y}{x}} = \ln{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}}$  可写为  $\arctan{\frac{y}{x}} = \frac{1}{2}\ln (x^2 +y^2)$  视  $y = y(x)$  ，上式两边对  $x$  求导得

$$
\frac {1}{1 + \left(\frac {y}{x}\right) ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {x y ^ {\prime} - y}{x ^ {2}} = \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {2 x + 2 y y ^ {\prime}}{x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}},
$$

即  $\frac{xy' - y}{x^2 + y^2} = \frac{x + yy'}{x^2 + y^2}$

所以  $y^\prime (x - y) = x + y$  ，推出  $\frac{dy}{dx} = y' = \frac{x + y}{x - y}\quad (x\neq y)$

16.【解析】令  $\sqrt{e^t - 1} = u$  ，则  $e^t = 1 + u^2, dt = \frac{2udu}{1 + u^2}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {\ln 2} ^ {2 \ln 2} \frac {1}{\sqrt {e ^ {t} - 1}} d t = \int_ {1} ^ {\sqrt {3}} \frac {2 u}{u (1 + u ^ {2})} \\ = 2 \int_ {1} ^ {\sqrt {3}} \frac {2 u}{u \left(1 + u ^ {2}\right)} d u \\ = 2 \arctan u \Big | _ {1} ^ {\sqrt {3}} \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
= 2 \left(\frac {\pi}{3} - \frac {\pi}{4}\right) = \frac {\pi}{6}
$$

19. 【解析】方程  $y'' + 4y' + 3y = 0$  的特征方程为

$$
\lambda^ {2} + 4 \lambda + 3 = 0
$$

解出  $\lambda_1 = -3, \lambda_2 = -1$

可知方程的通解为  $y = C_{1}e^{-3x} + C_{2}e^{-x}$

由上式可得  $y' = -3C_{1}e^{-3x} - C_{2}e^{-x}$

用初始条件  $y(0) = 2, y'(0) = 6$  代入上面两式得  $\left\{ \begin{array}{l} C_1 + C_2 = 2, \\ -3C_1 - C_2 = 6 \end{array} \right.$

解出  $C_1 = -4, C_2 = 6$  故所求的特解为  $y = -4e^{-3x} + 6e^{-x}$

$$

22. 【证明】设  $f(x) = \ln x$ ，则  $f^{\prime}(x) = \frac{1}{x}, x \in [t, t + 1]$

由拉格朗日中值定理知，存在一点  $\varsigma \in (t, t + 1)$ ，使

$f(1 + t) - f(t) = f^{\prime}(\varsigma)$  ，即  $\ln \left(1 + \frac{1}{t}\right) = \frac{1}{\varsigma},$

又因  $\frac{1}{1 + t} < \frac{1}{\varsigma} < \frac{1}{t}$ , 故  $\frac{1}{1 + t} < \ln \left(1 + \frac{1}{t}\right) < \frac{1}{t}$ .

23.【解析】应用分部积分法

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} (f (x) + f ^ {\prime \prime} (x)) \sin x d x = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} f (x) \sin x d x + f ^ {\prime} (x) \sin x \left| _ {0} ^ {\pi} - \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} f ^ {\prime} (x) \cos x d x \right. \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} f (x) \sin x d x - f (x) \cos x \left| _ {0} ^ {\pi} - \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} f (x) \sin x d x \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
= f (\pi) + f (0),
$$

由题意有  $f(\pi) + f(0) = 5, f(\pi) = 2$  ，所以  $f(0) = 3$
