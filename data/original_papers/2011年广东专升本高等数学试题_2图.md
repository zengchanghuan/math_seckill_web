11. 解：原式  $= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x - x(x + 1)}{x \sin x} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\cos x - 2x - 1}{\sin x + x \cos x}$  （3分）

$$
= \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {- \sin x - 2}{\cos x + \cos x - x \sin x} = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {- \sin x - 2}{2 \cos x - x \sin x} = - 1 \tag {6分}
$$

12. 解：  $f^{(n)}(x) = (\ln (\sqrt{1 + e^{-2x}} - e^{-x}))' = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 + e^{-2x}} - e^{-x}} (\sqrt{1 + e^{-2x}} - e^{-x})'$  (2 分)

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {1 + e ^ {- 2 x}} - e ^ {- x}} \left(\frac {- e ^ {- 2 x}}{\sqrt {1 + e ^ {- 2 x}}} + e ^ {- x}\right) = \frac {e ^ {- x}}{\sqrt {1 + e ^ {- 2 x}}}, (5分) \\ \therefore f ^ {(n)} (0) = \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} (6分) \\ \end{array}
$$

20. (1) 解法一:  $\frac{dy}{y} = (\cot x - \frac{1}{x})dx$ ,

$$
\therefore \int \frac {1}{y} d y = \int (\cot x - \frac {1}{x}) d x, \tag {2分}
$$

$$
\text {即} \ln y = \ln \sin x - \ln x + C _ {1}, y = \frac {C \sin x}{x}
$$

$$
\text {故 所 求 函 数 为} y = \frac {\sin x}{x} 。 \tag {5分}
$$

解法二:  $y' - (\cot x - \frac{1}{x})y = 0,$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \therefore y = C e ^ {\int (\cot x - \frac {1}{x}) d x} = C e ^ {\ln \sin x - \ln x} = \frac {C \sin x}{x} \quad (2 \text {分}) \\ \text {又} y \mid_ {x = \frac {\pi}{2}} = = \frac {2 C}{\pi}, \therefore C = 1 \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\text {故 所 求 函 数 为} y = \frac {\sin x}{x} \tag {5分}
$$

(2) 证:  $y' = \frac{x\cos x - \sin x}{x^2}$ , 令  $g(x) = x\cos x - \sin x$ , (6 分)

$$
\text {则} g ^ {\prime} (x) = \cos x - x \sin x - \cos x = - x \sin x
$$

$$
\text {当} x \in (0, \pi) \text {时 ,} g ^ {\prime} (x) <   0 \text {, 所 以} g (x) \text {在} (0, \pi) \text {内 单 调 递 减 ,}
$$

$$
\text {因 此 ， 当} x \in (0, \pi) \text {时 ， 有} g (x) <   g (0) = 0 \tag {8分}
$$
