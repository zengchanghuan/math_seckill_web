# 三、计算题（本大题共8小题，每小题6分，共48分）

11. 【解析】原式  $= \lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\int_0^x e^{t^2}dt - x}{x^3}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {e ^ {x ^ {2}} - 1}{3 x ^ {2}} \\ = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {2 x e ^ {x ^ {2}}}{6 x} = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {e ^ {x ^ {2}}}{3} = \frac {1}{3}. \\ \end{array}
$$

12. 【解析】  $f^{\prime}(0) = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(\Delta x) - f(0)}{\Delta x}$ ,

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \lim  _ {\Delta x \rightarrow 0} \frac {\Delta x \left(1 + 2 \Delta x ^ {2}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{\Delta x ^ {2}}} - 0}{\Delta x} = \lim  _ {\Delta x \rightarrow 0} \left(1 + 2 \Delta x ^ {2}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{\Delta x ^ {2}}} \\ = \lim  _ {\Delta x \rightarrow 0} [ (1 + 2 \Delta x ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {1}{2 \Delta x ^ {2}}} ] ^ {2} = e ^ {2}. \\ \end{array}
$$

13. 【解析】∵  $f^{\prime \prime}(x) = \ln (1 + x^{2}) + \frac{2x^{2}}{1 + x^{2}}$ ，

$$
f ^ {\prime \prime} (x) = \frac {2 x}{1 + x ^ {2}} + \frac {4 x \left(1 + x ^ {2}\right) - 4 x ^ {3}}{\left(1 + x ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} = \frac {2 x}{1 + x ^ {2}} + \frac {4 x}{\left(1 + x ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}}
$$

$$
\therefore f ^ {\prime \prime} (1) = 2.
$$

14. 【解析】设  $\sqrt{x} = t, x = t^2$ ，则

原式  $= \int \arctan t dt^2 = t^2 \arctan t - \int t^2 \frac{1}{1 + t^2} dt$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = t ^ {2} \arctan t - \int \left(1 - \frac {1}{1 + t ^ {2}}\right) d t = t ^ {2} \arctan t - t + \arctan t + C \\ = x \arctan \sqrt {x} - \sqrt {x} + \arctan \sqrt {x} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

15. 【解析】  $\because \frac{x^3}{1 + x^2}$  为奇函数，  $\therefore \int_{-1}^{1} \frac{x^3}{1 + x^2} dx = 0$

而  $\frac{|x|}{1 + x^2}$  为偶函数，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \therefore \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} \frac {| x |}{1 + x ^ {2}} d x = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {| x |}{1 + x ^ {2}} d x = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {x}{1 + x ^ {2}} d x \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {1}{1 + x ^ {2}} d \left(1 + x ^ {2}\right) = \ln \left(1 + x ^ {2}\right) \Big | _ {0} ^ {1} = \ln 2 \\ \end{array}
$$

故原式  $= \int_{-1}^{1}\frac{|x|}{1 + x^2} dx + \int_{-1}^{1}\frac{x^3}{1 + x^2} dx = \ln 2.$

16. 【解析】设  $F(x,y,z) = x^{y} + z^{3} + xz$  ，则

$$
F _ {x} ^ {\prime} = y x ^ {y - 1} + z, \quad F _ {y} ^ {\prime} = x ^ {y} \ln x, \quad F _ {z} ^ {\prime} = 3 z ^ {2} + x.
$$

所以  $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = -\frac{F_x'}{F_z'} = -\frac{yx^{y-1} + z}{3z^2 + x}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = -\frac{F_y'}{F_z'} = -\frac{x^y \ln x}{3z^2 + x}$ .

17. 【解析】设  $x = r\cos \theta, y = r\sin \theta$ ，

则原式  $= \int_{0}^{2\pi} d\theta \int_{1}^{2} (2r - 1)^{3} dr = 2\pi \int_{1}^{2} (2r - 1)^{3} dr$

$$
= \frac {\pi}{4} (2 r - 1) ^ {4} \Bigg | _ {1} ^ {2} = \frac {8 1 \pi}{4} - \frac {\pi}{4} = 2 0 \pi
$$

18. 【解析】因为微分方程的特征方程为  $r^2 + r - 6 = 0$

解得  $r_1 = -3, r_2 = 2$

$\therefore$  微分方程的通解为  $y = c_{1}e^{-3x} + c_{2}e^{2x}$

$$
\because y ^ {\prime} = - 3 c _ {1} e ^ {- 3 x} + 2 c _ {2} e ^ {2 x},
$$

有  $y \big|_{x=0} = c_1 + c_2 = 1$

$\left.y^{\prime}\right|_{x = 0} = -3c_{1} + 2c_{2} = -8$  ，解得  $c_{1} = 2$  ，  $c_{2} = -1$

故特解为  $y = 2e^{-3x} - e^{2x}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \left. \left(e ^ {y} + \frac {1}{2} y ^ {2} - y\right) \right| _ {0} ^ {1} = e + \frac {1}{2} - 1 - 1 \\ = e - \frac {3}{2}. \\ \end{array}

$$

20. 【解析】（1） $\because f'(x) = 2x + 4 - 4\ln x - 4 = 2x - 4\ln x, f''(x) = 2 - \frac{4}{x}$ ,

当  $0 < x < 2$  时， $f''(x) < 0$ ，所以  $f(x)$  在（0，2）上的图形是凸的。

（2）：当  $0 < x < 2$  时， $f''(x) < 0$

$\therefore f^{\prime}(x)$  在（0，2]上单调减少，由此知：

当  $0 < x < 2$  时，有  $f^{\prime}(x) > f^{\prime}(2) = 4 - 4\ln 2 > 0,$

故  $f(x)$  在区间（0，2]上单调增加

因此当  $0 < x < 2$  时，有

$$
f (x) <   f (2) = 4 + 8 - 8 \ln 2 - 8 = 4 - 8 \ln 2 = 4 - 4 \ln 4 <   0.
$$
